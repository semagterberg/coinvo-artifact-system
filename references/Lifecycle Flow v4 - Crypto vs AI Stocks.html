Cooking rule · override across all 6 stages
Never interfere with a post that's actively running, even to "improve" it. Edits, deletes, and mid-run replies redirect the algorithm and break the lifecycle at whatever stage they fire.

Stages 1–4 fire on view-count thresholds (0, 50K, 150K, 1M). Stages 5–6 fire on time-based windows that only open once a 1M winner is logged. Crossing 1M is the only one-way gate.
