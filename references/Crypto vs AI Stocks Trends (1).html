Side-by-side comparison of two business models. On the left, the creator model as a profile card for Jake Reynolds, @jakereynolds, with bio about crypto trading and market takes. The product is the person. Reader thinks: what does Jake think today. Authority lives in the person. On the right, the distribution model as Coinvo's browser feed at x.com/bycoinvo, with three news cards: breaking news on China dumping US Treasuries, AI news on GPT-5.5 shipping 10M context window, and markets news on BTC reclaiming 98K with ETF inflows hitting 12B. The product is the feed. Reader thinks: what is happening right now. Authority lives in the feed. Closing: we are not building a face, we are building the page people check.
